How to Prepare Shiro Stew

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in 2 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute.
  4. Add 1 cup of Adet Shiro powder and gradually stir in 2 cups of water.
  5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the stew thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with injera bread or rice.

Pro Tip: For a richer flavor, add a tablespoon of niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced butter) while cooking.
